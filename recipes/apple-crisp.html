Apple Crisp Recipe

Description

This simple but tasty apple crisp is one you'll definitely need to try! This top rated recipe is one to shock those at your dinner tonight.

Ingredients

  • 10 cups of peeled, cored, and sliced apples
  • 1 cup water sugar
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup quick-cooking oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
